# Send Keys
## Two Methods to Send Keys:
[Windows.Forms.SendKeys]::SendWait('hello')

$wshell = New-Object -ComObject Wscript.Shell;
$wshell.sendkeys("hi")

## Key Formats (https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/api/system.windows.forms.sendkeys?view=netframework-4.7.2)
{up} {down} {right} {left} | Arrows
{bksp} {break} {capslock} {del} {end} 
{enter} {esc} {help} {home} {insert} 
{pgdn} {pgup} {prtsc} {scrolllock} {tab}
{F1} {add} {subtract} {multiply}
{divide} {numlock}
+ | shift
^ | ctrl 
% | alt
{LEFT 42} | press left 42 times 
+(EC) | Hold down shift while..
